How to delete your location

You can delete added location by swipping from left to right a location you want to delete:

Weather details

You can select previously bookmarked location by pressing one from the list. You can see the current weather details: weather image, current temperature, humidity, wind speed, wind degree and rain volume for the last 3 hours (if available). You can see 5 days forecast with weather image, maximum daily temperature for next 5 days:

Settings

There is an option to remove all places you've added in settings. You can configure units here, too. Metic will show temperature in °C and speed in m/s; imperial will show temperature in °F and speed in mph.
